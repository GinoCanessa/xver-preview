Resource Profile: ProfileBodyStructure

0.0.1-snapshot-3
Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/5.0/StructureDefinition/profile-BodyStructure Version:
Standards status: Trial-use Active as of 2026-02-09 Maturity Level: 0 Computable Name: ProfileBodyStructure
