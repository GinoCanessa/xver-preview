Logical Definition (CLD)

  • Include these codes as defined in http://hl7.org/fhir/cited-artifact-status-type version 📍5.0.0
    CodeDisplayDefinition
    acceptedAcceptedThe content that was not published yet has been approved for publication by the publisher and/or editor.
    activeActiveThe content is considered complete for its current state by the content creator.
    approvedApprovedThe content has been approved for a state transition, with the focus of approval described in the text associated with this coding.
    archivedArchivedThe content is retired or considered no longer current but still available as part of the public record.
    createdCreatedThe content was originally constructed or composed.
    draftDraftThe content is considered unfinished or incomplete and not representative of the current state desired by the content creator.
    post-review-pre-publishedPost review pre publishedThe content is in a state between the review(s) being completed and being published.
    pre-reviewPre reviewThe content is awaiting assignment and delivery to reviewer(s).
    published-early-formPublished early formThe content is published but future changes to the published version are expected.
    published-final-formPublished final formThe content is published and further changes to the content are not expected.
    rejectedRejectedThe content that was not published has been removed from consideration for publishing by a publisher or editor.
    retractedRetractedThe content that was published is removed from publication and should no longer be considered part of the public record.
    submittedSubmittedThe content was sent to the publisher for consideration of publication.
    under-reviewUnder reviewThe content is in a state of being reviewed.
    unknownUnknownThe status of the content is not recorded in the metadata.
    withdrawnWithdrawnThe content that was not published has been removed from consideration for publishing by the submitter.
