CodeSystem: SubstanceAdminSubstitution

2.1.0
Official URL: http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/v3-substanceAdminSubstitution Version:
Standards status: Trial-use Active as of 2019-03-19 Maturity Level: 0 Computable Name: SubstanceAdminSubstitution

Identifies what sort of change is permitted or has occurred between the therapy that was ordered and the therapy that was/will be provided.
