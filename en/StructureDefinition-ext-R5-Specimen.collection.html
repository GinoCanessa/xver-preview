Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/5.0/StructureDefinition/extension-Specimen.collection Version: 0.0.1-snapshot-3
Standards status: Trial-use Active as of 2026-02-17 Maturity Level: 0 Computable Name: ExtensionSpecimen_Collection

This cross-version extension represents the FHIR R5 element Specimen.collection for use in FHIR R4.

Context of Use

    This extension may be used on the following element(s)

    • Element ID: Specimen.collection

    This extension is allowed for use with FHIR versions R4 to R4 .

Summary

Complex Extension: This cross-version extension represents the FHIR R5 element Specimen.collection for use in FHIR R4.

  • collector: Reference: Person who collected the specimen.
  • collected: dateTime, Period: Time when specimen was collected from subject - the physiologically relevant time.
  • duration: Duration: The span of time over which the collection of a specimen occurred.
  • quantity: Quantity: The quantity of specimen collected; for instance the volume of a blood sample, or the physical measurement of an anatomic pathology sample.
  • method: CodeableConcept: A coded value specifying the technique that is used to perform the procedure.
  • _datatype: string: Slice to indicate the presence of a R5 CodeableReference in FHIR R4
  • concept: CodeableConcept: A reference to a concept - e.g. the information is identified by its general class to the degree of precision found in the terminology.
  • reference: Reference: A reference to a resource the provides exact details about the information being referenced.
  • procedure: Reference: The procedure event during which the specimen was collected (e.g. the surgery leading to the collection of a pathology sample).
  • _datatype: string: Slice to indicate the presence of a R5 CodeableReference in FHIR R4
  • concept: CodeableConcept: A reference to a concept - e.g. the information is identified by its general class to the degree of precision found in the terminology.
  • reference: Reference: A reference to a resource the provides exact details about the information being referenced.
  • fastingStatus: CodeableConcept, Duration: Abstinence or reduction from some or all food, drink, or both, for a period of time prior to sample collection.

Summary

Complex Extension: This cross-version extension represents the FHIR R5 element Specimen.collection for use in FHIR R4.

  • collector: Reference: Person who collected the specimen.
  • collected: dateTime, Period: Time when specimen was collected from subject - the physiologically relevant time.
  • duration: Duration: The span of time over which the collection of a specimen occurred.
  • quantity: Quantity: The quantity of specimen collected; for instance the volume of a blood sample, or the physical measurement of an anatomic pathology sample.
  • method: CodeableConcept: A coded value specifying the technique that is used to perform the procedure.
  • _datatype: string: Slice to indicate the presence of a R5 CodeableReference in FHIR R4
  • concept: CodeableConcept: A reference to a concept - e.g. the information is identified by its general class to the degree of precision found in the terminology.
  • reference: Reference: A reference to a resource the provides exact details about the information being referenced.
  • procedure: Reference: The procedure event during which the specimen was collected (e.g. the surgery leading to the collection of a pathology sample).
  • _datatype: string: Slice to indicate the presence of a R5 CodeableReference in FHIR R4
  • concept: CodeableConcept: A reference to a concept - e.g. the information is identified by its general class to the degree of precision found in the terminology.
  • reference: Reference: A reference to a resource the provides exact details about the information being referenced.
