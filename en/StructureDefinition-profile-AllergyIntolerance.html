Resource Profile: ProfileAllergyIntolerance

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/5.0/StructureDefinition/profile-AllergyIntolerance Version: 0.0.1-snapshot-3
Standards status: Trial-use Active as of 2026-02-21 Maturity Level: 0 Computable Name: ProfileAllergyIntolerance

This cross-version profile allows R5 AllergyIntolerance content to be represented via FHIR R4 AllergyIntolerance resources.

Usages:
